FAQs

Common questions.

Do you teach fully remote?

Yes — all sessions are conducted via video call. I work with students across the U.S. and internationally, with scheduling built around evenings and weekends to accommodate different time zones.

How quickly can we start?

Typically within a few days of our initial consult. The first session is a diagnostic and goal-setting call, so there's no prep required on your end before we meet.

Do you assign homework?

Yes — practice between sessions is essential. Assignments are targeted and specific: usually a short problem set or timed drill tied directly to what we covered. Quality over quantity.

Can you coordinate with a teacher or parent?

Absolutely. I'm happy to align with classroom teachers on curriculum pacing or share progress reports with parents. Clear communication is part of the process.
